BAYCO

Bay Area Youth Career Opportunities (BAYCO) is a student-run organization that strives to propel career-related success for high schoolers in the Bay Area through educational workshops, professional development, and networking opportunities.

Past Events

Mock Interview

Students were able to bring their resumes and participate in multiple mock interviews from volunteers who work in industry.

Job Fair

We invited 27 businesses in numerous fields such as technology, drama, and athletics to offer jobs, internships, and volunteer opportunities to more than 200 students.

Apple Event

In partnership with the Apple Company, we hosted a college and career pathways fair, offering students the opportunity to understand what different departments do. Resume review workshops and Q&A panels were held as well.
